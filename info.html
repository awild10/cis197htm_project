What instruments do you teach?

Guitar at all skill levels, and piano and ukulele from beginner through intermediate. Lessons can also include music theory, improvisation and composition/songwriting!

How long are lessons?

Students usually start with 30 minutes every week, and 45 and 60 minute lessons are also an option.

Where do lessons take place?

I offer in home and online video lessons.
